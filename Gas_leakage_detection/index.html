Gas Leakage Detection Project

Introduction

Indicates the danger to people in workplaces, factories, and homes. Gas leakage is detected using an Arduino with an MQ2 Gas Sensor.

Project Highlights

  • Gas leak detection systems can shut down the gas supply to minimize the risk of fire breakouts and explosions.
  • LPG safety measures in kitchen gas cylinders.
  • Automatic LPG Gas Leakage Detection System using Arduino.
